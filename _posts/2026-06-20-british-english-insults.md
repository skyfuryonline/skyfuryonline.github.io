<p class="bowen-intro">这是一段 30 秒的英式英语对骂对话。两个人互相用各种英国特色损人词汇攻击对方，语气激烈但用词相当"讲究"——比起美式英语的粗口，英式 insults 更有一种"优雅的刻薄"。</p>

## 对话原文

> **A:** Hey, shut up! You absolute **wally**.
> **B:** Oh, listen to yourself, you bloody **plonker**.
> **A:** You're a **muppet**, and you're a walking disaster.
> **B:** You **cheeky sod**. Better than being a **useless lump**. You're **pathetic**.
> **A:** That's rich, coming from a **coward**.
> **B:** Say that again.
> **A:** Gladly. **Coward**. You're a **hypocrite**. And you're a **spineless coward**.
> **B:** Don't lecture me, you **nasty piece of work**.
> **A:** Someone has to. You just can't face what you are.
> **B:** You make me sick. Shut your mouth.

<div class="bowen-highlight">
  <strong>📝 学习笔记：英式 vs 美式损人</strong>
  <p>英式 insults 和美式有明显区别：</p>
  <ul>
    <li>英式更倾向于用"创意词汇"来损人（<span class="bowen-kw">wally</span>、<span class="bowen-kw">plonker</span>、<span class="bowen-kw">muppet</span>），而不是直接的 F-word</li>
    <li><span class="bowen-kw">bloody</span> 是英式经典语气词，相当于"该死的"，在正式场合仍属轻度粗口</li>
    <li><span class="bowen-kw">sod</span> 在英式英语里很常见，但在美式英语中很少用</li>
    <li>"That's rich, coming from..." 是很地道的英式反驳句式，意思是"你还有脸说别人"</li>
    <li>整体来说，英式 banter（互损）是一种社交文化，朋友间互骂 <span class="bowen-kw">plonker</span> 可能是表示亲近</li>
  </ul>
